Am•bas•sa•dor

noun /amˈbasədər/

An ambassador represents and promotes something they are passionate about, whether it be an initiative, an organization, a field, etc. Their goal is to increase awareness and exposure to certain topics of interest, targeting a wide audience, so that these individuals too can develop a passion.

What you do

The ambassadors serve as the representatives of the Meducation Foundation. They are the front-end of the organization and a vital part of the development of all Meducation events. Be a part of planning and promotion of initiatives including conferences, fundraisers, and consultation services.

Benefits

Meducation Foundation ambassadors gain insight into the world of medicine through access to unique experiences and social networking opportunities. Experience personal development by helping others in the community.
