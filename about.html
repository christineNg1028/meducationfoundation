Meducation Foundation

The Meducation Mission

Meducation Foundation aims to provide opportunities, resources, and platforms for students of all backgrounds - we believe that all students deserve equal footing in their academic journey towards medicine and we work towards establishing accessibility and transparency throughout this process.
